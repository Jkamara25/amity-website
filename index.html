About

What is Amity?

Amity is a collective alias: many individuals use a single loyalty identity, generating contradictory data trails that destabilise supermarket profiling systems.

Anonymity

Amity conceals the individual behind a shared customer mask.

Collectivity

Points, rewards and identity are pooled and redistributed.

Disruption

Behavioural prediction breaks down when one person becomes many.

The Amity Collective

Those who choose to share the identity shape how Amity behaves.

Rules
  • Anyone may “be” Amity temporarily.
  • No single owner. No fixed self.
  • Rewards decided by committee or randomness.
Experiments
  • Ghost receipts
  • Fruit sticker interventions
  • Random reward allocation
  • Poster subvertising
Aims
  • Expose data extraction
  • Break behavioural profiling
  • Reimagine shared identity
Join

Join Amity

Joining Amity means taking part in a speculative system of shared identity.

  • Receive or access an Amity card.
  • Scan as Amity when you shop.
  • Your data merges into the collective profile.
  • Rewards redistributed through the collective.

This is a speculative design project, not a functional service.
